OUR MISSION

Our mission is to create a learning platform where every member gets an opportunity to aquire & develop the skills in semiconductor domain for pursuing a career in this field as well as for life-long learning.

OUR VALUES

OUR OBJECTIVES

To conduct activities to create awareness about career opportunities in the core VLSI domain.

To organize workshops/seminars/guest lectures on industry relevant topics in the VLSI domain.

To collaborate with VLSI companies/industries to upgrade the skillsets of all the stakeholders and to explore project/internship opportunities.

To actively participate in VLSI related technical events/competitions.

To provide placement related assistance in association with the Training & Placement Office.

To create a global network of NIE alumni and an ecosystem for them to contribute to the Club.
